---
title: Flood risk perceptions in Florida cities
date: 2024-10-24
tags: [flooding, adaptation, risk-perception]
---

This study compares how residents in Hyde Park (Tampa) and Saint Augustine perceive flood risk and choose adaptation measures. It contrasts neighborhood-level drivers of awareness, willingness to act, and the perceived effectiveness of interventions.
